explained for genuine understanding and interview readiness, organized by subject and
tied together with curated learning paths. Every note aims to teach the transferable
idea, the "why" and the trade-offs, not just a definition.

## How it's organized

Notes live under **durable subjects** (folders like `software-architecture`,
`web-development`, `security`), not under the lesson that produced them, so the library
grows by adding rather than reshuffling. **Maps (MOCs)** like the ones above curate paths
through the notes. Everything is cross-linked with wikilinks, so the graph and backlinks
surface how ideas connect.
